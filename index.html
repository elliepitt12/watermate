ABOUT US



Our company Aqua Bloom will be illustrating and creating a smart automated irrigation water system called Watermate that will determine when to water the soil of farmers crops based on its current moisture and humidity levels.

This will help specifically farmers in poorer countries such as India for them to be able to produce higher quality crops for less amount of money as our system will be affordable for these people. It will help avoid crop failure due to excess or lack of water supply going to the farmers crops.

Our product will be automated so that when the soil is moist enough for the crops it will turn off automatically by itself through the moisture sensor. Data will be collected from the sensors and the hardware will be visible for our user for data analysis and future data prediction for the farmers to use.

There will also be a manual setting for the pump that will allow the user themselves to turn the pump on and off through the website. For this we will be providing three different languages such as English, Spanish, and Hindi. The main goal will be to help farmers grow their crops to make them healthier and of higher quality.
